I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

A highschool boy finds his crush's diary on a table in a hospital. In the notebook he discovers that she is suffering from a fatal pancreatic disease. From that point on, he decides to make the most of her final days. The romantic, tear-jerking story sees the two characters brought closer and closer together in a beautiful and harrowing tale.
